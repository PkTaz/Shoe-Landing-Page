Graduating at De Montfort University with a masters in fashion and design. Havarti has spent years working alongside A-List celebrities such as Kanye West, Tom Cruise, Elton John, and many more. For this years elemental shoes Havarti said he was estatic to be asked to work on the fire shoes and deliver some of the most stylish and comfortable shoes of the year.

With his brand new single dropping Straight Outta Milk Paul Pearson has been working alongside Sophia Webster to also bring you this years elemental shoes earth design. Paul has been a huge fan of sHOTs Shoes and said it has been a blessing to work with some of the most amazing shoe designers in the industry.

For 30 year's Jamie's fashion mind has been taking over the world. The two-time grammy award winner was selected for this years air design and has showcased her fashion skills very well. Jamie says she was inspired by the sheer art of nature and would like to express how important it is to go outside and be one with the wind. This year's air shoes have nearly doubled in sales to previous years and Jamie would like to thank her success to her family.

Growing up on the streets at a young age Dill was forced to grow up fast and make money for himself. Ever since Dill was 10 he was able to create shoes from what little resources he had. From bags and wood to rubber bands and plastic Dill found ways to persevere and show the world it is possible. Dill was selected to create this year's water shoes and has shocked the world with one of the most creative designs we have ever seen. Dill would like to give his thanks to the small group of friends that never stopped believing in his passion and dreams for the art of shoes.
